AKAN Names(Ghana)

This projects will be build as a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. This Akan Website display names for a baby girl/boy right after birthday by taking his birthday dates and his gender and they get his/her name in Akan. Those names are named after days in Akan,and every possess a name corresponding to the day he/she was given birth.
